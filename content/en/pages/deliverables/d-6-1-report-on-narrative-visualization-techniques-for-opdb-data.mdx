This document focuses on the state of the art of digital storytelling solutions for object-oriented prosopography data (OPDB), the new InTaVia novel data type. It explains the notion of visualisation-based storytelling and collects best practices from related storytelling surveys published in the visualisation domain. It also describes the best practice solutions for the story entities (person, object, set, historical event, place) and categorises them according to the design space criteria.

This deliverable is classified as **public**.
