This document describes the dissemination activities carried out during the first 18 months of the project, following the procedure described in the Dissemination Plan (Deliverable 8.2 and its first update in Deliverable 8.4). In addition, it shows how, to what extent and how often the website, newsletter and social media were/are used to amplify the InTaVia message and results. It is important to note that this document is an interim report on InTaVia’s communication and dissemination activities; the outreach and dissemination activities' impact will be fully assessed in the final report.

This deliverable is currently classified as **confidential** and is not available for download.
