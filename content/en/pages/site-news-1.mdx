Mid of July 2020, the European Commission invited the INTAVIA consortium to prepare a grant agreement. According to the call “Curation of digital assets and advanced digitisation” H2020-SC6-TRANSFORMATIONS-2018-2019-2020 ([link](https://ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders/opportunities/portal/screen/opportunities/topic-details/dt-transformations-12-2018-2020)) the INTAVIA proposal suggested to develop an integrated approach to the analysis, curation and communicaiton of Europe’s tangible and intangible cultural assets.
