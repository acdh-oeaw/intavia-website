**Innovation workshop.** We will held an open innovation workshop in February 2021 with 41 participants from cultural heritage research and practice. We collected relevant user practices associated with tangible cultural assets and intangible biographical information for the definition of user requirements.

**Kick-off meeting.** The project kick-off meeting took place on November 19th, 2020. see [report](http://intavia.eu/site-news-2/).
