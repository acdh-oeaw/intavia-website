The **InTaVia team** draws from nine European institutions dedicated to research and development in the area of digital cultural heritage.

* [**Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam**](#vu) (Netherlands)

* [**Research Centre of the Slovenian Academy of Sciences & Arts** ](#zrcsazu)(Slovenia)

* [**University of Southern Denmark**](#sdu) (Denmark)

* [**Austrian Academy of Sciences**](#oeaw) (Austria)

* [**University of Stuttgart**](#ustutt) (Germany)

* [**Fluxguide** ](#fluxguide)(Austria)
